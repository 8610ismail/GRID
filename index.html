ART GALLERY

ABOUT

One of the benefits of art galleries is it can help widen your knowledge about art. You do not only learn art education from school. An art gallery is a good place to actually study it. You can join guided tours where you can get valuable information about the displayed artworks and tips on how to view a piece of art. You can also participate in workshops where you can have hands-on experience in painting or sculpting your artwork. There are also lectures conducted by artists where you can ask questions during a question-and-answer session. You will find all these activities very informative. Nami Art Gallery has knowledgeable staff who can give guidance and information about the artworks in the gallery.
